MoreInfo

This Presentation has 4 main slides and one about the visualization slide. There is a next button between each slide that takes the user to the next visualization. Slide 1 gives the user a brief intro to the project and gives some details about the visualization. From here the user understands what the visualization is about and what he/she can do to interact with the charts. The interactive slideshow starts off with a career overview and some basic information of salah. I used a grouped bar chart to indicate the number of goals, appearences and assists he had in each competition. The slide is meant to give an observer some preliminary information about the player. I tried to arrange the graph from the cups he scores least in to the cups he scores most in to make it easy to visualize. the second slide is an Interactive slide. The User now can understand more about how salah is as a player. Moreover, they can study about his 17-18 season, which was his breakthrough season. The author can now either move onto the next slide by hitting next or choose one of the buttons to see a grouped bar chart visualization. The first visualization is his performance by position. It shows how well he plays based on the different positions he has been put it. The second one on the other hand shows his performance by competition. I tried to keep the legend consistant with that of the previous graph, So it would be easier for people to transition. The third slide talks about salah's market value over the years. Salah's value has significantly gone up over the years. Here a key part of the data is highlighted which indicated the increase in revenue from 2014 onwards when he started playing for the bigger clubs. More importantly the highlighted region shows the steep rise in market value in 17-18. The 4th and final slide has a year by year comparision (of the last 3 years) between salah and two of the best epl players - Harry Kane and Eden hazard. The user can choose which year he/she wants to view. while toggling the year the graph changes. The information below th graph also changes. It is a good way for the user to understand the consistency levels of Mohamed Salah.
